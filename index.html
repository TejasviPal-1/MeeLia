Did You Know?

Over 44 million American adults have a mental health condition

Yet 1 in 5 adults report having unmet mental health needs

~ ~ Mental Health in America, 2015

MeeLia aims to bridge that gap by bringing accessible mental health help to those that need it, thus reducing the waiting times for patients to access help. It uses machine learning to perform sentimental analysis of conversations with users to empower patients.
